About IIC

OUR VISION

IIT Dharwad Institute Innovation Council (IIC) is established to conduct various innovation, entrepreneurship related activities and to systematically foster the culture of innovation in IIT Dharwad. Currently, the council consists of 15 student members, 4 faculty members and 2 external members, with Prof. Raj Hirwani as the President.
