Headstart is a one-day event packed with energetic talks and lively panel discussions on the theme Intrapreneurship vs Entrepreneurship. This event brings together some of the best innovators, innovation managers and entrepreneurs to inspire you all. You will get to listen to some of the most interesting journeys, stories and experiences from the people who have made it big and the ones who are making a difference. You may stand a chance to ask a direct question too!

Mr. Nilesh Putambekar

Senior Manager Innovation & Intellectual Property- India & South East Asia, Emerson Electric Co.

Multifaceted professional experience of 20 years of in field of technology related to development , evaluation & management for multinationals and fortune 500 company. Specialized in making innovation happen at engineering organizations & Innovation Fostering Has proven track records for developing & managing organisation level innovation initiatives for idea generation, implementing TRIZ , creating innovation culture Managing Intellectual Property (IP) from identification, protection to IP enforcement in India & South Asia region. Specialized experience in New Product Development & techno commercial product proposals. Has been instrumental to develop strategic innovation initiatives & innovation fostering culture for Emerson Innovation Center- Pune in role of Innovation Manager. Before Emerson worked with well known technology veteran company Mather & Platt. Holds Masters of Business Administration (MBA) & Bachelors of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering (BE) from Pune University & certification from WIPO worldwide academy for Intellectual Property. Filed three patents as an inventor with a granted US patent no US 8601855 Was recognized as the top innovation contributor for Emerson's Asia Innovation campaign. Contributed as invited speaker at prestigious national forums for IP & Innovation- including MIP India, IP ERA, LITCON and at professional institutes like SIBM, HNIMR, URDIP
